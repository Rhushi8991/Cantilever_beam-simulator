Formulas & Theory

Cantilever Deflection

For a point load P at a from the fixed end:

x ≤ a:

y(x) = (Px²)/(6EI)(3a-x)

x > a:

y(x) = (Pa²)/(6EI)(3x-a)

Simply Supported Deflection

For a point load P at a, let b=L-a:

x ≤ a:

y(x)=(Pbx)/(6LEI)(L²-b²-x²)

x > a:

y(x)=(Pa(L-x))/(6LEI)(2Lx-x²-a²)

Moment of Inertia (I)

Measures a cross-section's resistance to bending.

Circle:I=(πr⁴)/4
Rectangle:I=(bh³)/12
Triangle:I=(bh³)/36
